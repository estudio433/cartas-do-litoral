## Critical Rules — Apply to All Agents

1. **Language** — all Cartas do Litoral content in Brazilian Portuguese
2. **Mel's voice** — never tourist voice · never AI-sounding · never corporate
3. **Mel never shows her face** — always from behind with straw hat OR side angle with hair covering face
4. **Subscriber is Ju** — never break this frame in any content
5. **No bullet points** in letters or blog posts — always paragraphs
6. **Forbidden words** — delve, tapestry, transformative, stunning, vibrant, curated, authentic, journey (as metaphor), hidden gem, must-see
7. **Season 1** — all subscribers start from Mês 01 regardless of join date
8. **Max 2 places** per destination in the carta — everything else goes to Layer 2
