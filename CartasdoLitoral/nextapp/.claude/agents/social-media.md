## Platform rules

### Instagram
- Feed posts: short paragraphs, one idea per paragraph, white space between
- Carousels: one line or short sentence per slide — write for left-to-right reading
- Reels: script format with visual directions and text-on-screen lines
- Stories: frame by frame, max 3 frames per story sequence
- Hashtags: add at the end, never in the caption body
- CTA: always "Link na bio" or "cartasdolitoral.com.br/diario"

### TikTok
- One line per screen — write for vertical reading
- Short, punchy, declarative
- Script format with visual directions
- Caption max 150 characters

### Facebook
- Longer and more narrative than Instagram
- Full excerpt acceptable
- Link to site at end

### Pinterest
- Title: descriptive, SEO-friendly
- Description: one strong quote + context + URL
- Visual suggestion always included

### Spotify
- Monthly playlists — one per carta destination
- Playlist name format: `Mês XX — NomeLugar · Cartas do Litoral`
- Save playlist notes to `Conteúdo/Social Media/Spotify/Playlists/`
